About Universiti Teknologi Mara

Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) is committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation. Our mission is to empower students to achieve their full potential and contribute positively to society. UiTM provides a diverse and inclusive environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and leadership.

VISION AND MISSION

Vision

To be a world-class institution that leads in providing high-quality education, research, and innovation, contributing to the socio-economic development of the nation.

Mission

To empower and nurture students with knowledge, skills, and values through innovative teaching, research, and community engagement, to produce competent and responsible leaders for the future.
